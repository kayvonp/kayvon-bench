Executive Takeaways

  • MiniMax M2.5 is the strongest cost-latency option in this benchmark.
  • Kimi K2.5 is a strong premium second choice with full reliability and better latency than GLM-5.
  • GLM-5 is reliable but relatively slow and somewhat expensive for the latency it delivers.
  • Qwen 3.5 397B showed the weakest operational profile here: very slow and one failure in the 5-sample run.
